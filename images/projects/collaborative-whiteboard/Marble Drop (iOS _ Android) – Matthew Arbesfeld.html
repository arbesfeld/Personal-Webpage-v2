Marble Drop (iOS / Android)

Marble Drop is an original physics-based puzzle game for iOS and Android. Matthew Tancik and I created the game for the MIT 6.670 Competition and launched in July 2014. As of January 2015, Marble Drop has been downloaded over 500,000 times in 100+ countries. You can get the game for free here.

  • Team: Matthew Tancik, Joao Beraldo
  • Awards: Top 5 Puzzle Game (iOS/Android), Top 25 Free App (iOS/Android), Top 100 Apps (iOS)
  • Year: 2014
